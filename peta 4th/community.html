Top 5 Community Issues

  1. Traffic Congestion along Gen. Luna Avenue The heavy volume of vehicles during rush hours continues to cause long delays for daily commuters. Residents are calling for better traffic management and road widening projects.
  2. Waste Management and Proper Disposal Improper garbage segregation in some neighborhoods has led to clogged drainage systems. Local officials are pushing for stricter enforcement of "No Segregation, No Collection" rules.
  3. Seasonal Flooding in Low-Lying Areas Communities near the river remain concerned about rising water levels during the rainy season. Ongoing dredging and dike improvements are currently the top priority for safety.
  4. Public Street Lighting and Safety Several inner roads lack sufficient lighting, making it difficult for pedestrians to walk safely at night. Barangay leaders are working to install more solar-powered street lamps.
  5. Access to Youth Learning Centers Many students are requesting more public Wi-Fi zones and quiet study spaces within the town. Expanding these hubs would greatly support local education and research.

✦ DID YOU KNOW? ✦

Local Statistic: San Mateo is currently one of the fastest-growing municipalities in Rizal, with its population nearly doubling over the last two decades.
Historical Fact: Founded in 1572, San Mateo is one of the oldest towns in the Philippines and played a vital role during the 1896 Revolution.
Fun Fact: Our town is officially known as the "Kakanin Capital" of Rizal because we produce the most delicious native rice cakes in the region!
