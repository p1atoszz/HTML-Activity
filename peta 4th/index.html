Welcome to Soria Times, a student digital newspaper highlighting important stories from San Mateo, Rizal. This project focuses on local events, community concerns, and future possibilities for the town. By sharing information and ideas, the newspaper encourages residents to stay informed and involved in their community.

In this edition, readers will find updates on local development, community issues, and youth programs. There is also an opinion section discussing a common issue in the town and a feature about how San Mateo could develop in the future.
