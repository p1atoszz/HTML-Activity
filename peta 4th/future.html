Our Community in 2035

By 2035, San Mateo could become a model of innovation and sustainability. Imagine smart classrooms in every school, equipped with interactive technology and AI-assisted learning to enhance student performance. Youth programs would be more abundant, giving students opportunities in sports, arts, and leadership.

Transportation will be more efficient, with electric buses and improved road networks reducing travel time. Residents can access digital government services from home, from paying taxes to filing permits, making local governance more transparent and convenient.

With these developments, San Mateo would be a vibrant town where residents, especially the youth, are empowered to learn, participate, and innovate. Together, community and government efforts can turn these dreams into reality.
