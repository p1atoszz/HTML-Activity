Duwende

The Duwende is a small, goblin-like creature from Philippine mythology that lives underground, specifically in termite mounds called punso. They are known as the guardians of the land and are famous for being invisible to the human eye. In our culture, they teach us to respect nature. Always remember to say "Tabi-tabi po" when passing by their homes!

Legend Teaser

"In the middle of our busy cities and concrete homes, something older remains. We build over their mounds and ignore the whispers in the garden. But when the lights flicker and the systems fail, we remember the one rule we forgot: Tabi-tabi po."
