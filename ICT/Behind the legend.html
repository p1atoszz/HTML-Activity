Behind the Legend

Origin of the Myth

The Duwende is a staple of Philippine folklore, heavily influenced by both pre-colonial animism and Spanish myths (the duende). They are believed to be tiny, supernatural beings that live in mounds, trees, or even dark corners of houses. They are categorized by colorâ€”White and Green duwendes are seen as lucky or friendly, while Black duwendes are mischievous or malevolent.


Reflection

Creating this PT allowed me to reconnect with the stories my grandparents used to tell me to keep me from wandering too far into the woods. To me, the Duwende represents the Filipino people's deep-rooted respect for nature and the unseen world. Even in our modern age, the phrase 'Tabi-tabi po' serves as a beautiful reminder to be humble and mindful of our surroundings. This legend teaches us that our actions have consequences, especially when we disturb the balance of the environment. I hope this website helps keep this enchanting part of our culture alive for my classmates.
