Beginning

In a quiet subdivision in Laguna, Marco was excited to clear his backyard for a new garden project. In the corner near an old fence stood a small, mossy mound of earth. Ignoring his grandmother's warnings to say "Tabi-tabi po," Marco viewed it as an eyesore. He took his shovel and leveled the mound, laughing at the thought of spirits living in a pile of dirt.

Middle

By nightfall, the air in the house grew heavy and cold. Marco suddenly collapsed with a violent fever that no paracetamol could break. His skin felt like it was burning from the inside, and his legs began to swell until he could no longer stand. In the silence of the night, his family heard tiny, scratching sounds coming from beneath the floor tiles of the new garden room.
