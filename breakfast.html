Ingredients â†¯

4 pcs eggplants, 2 pcs eggs, 1 pc Knorr Chicken Cube, crushed

Steps

  1. Boil eggplants in a medium pot until tender. Drain and let cool. Peel skin and flatten flesh using a fork.
  2. Beat eggs in a bowl. Add Knorr Chicken Cube and mix until fully dissolved.
  3. Heat a pan over medium flame. Dip eggplant in egg mixture, then fry until golden brown.
